About me

I am a Scholar at the Forrest Research Foundation working at Curtin University. I work in planetary science. I study the geochemistry of meteorites to learn about the interiors of asteroids.

I am currently investigating the process of differentiation and core formation in regions of the outer solar system by conducting melting experiments on meteorites.

Research interests

  • Planetary science
  • Meteorite geochemistry
  • Currently researching differentiation and core formation in the outer solar system
